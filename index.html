About Me

Xing Di (底兴) is working with Prof. Vishal M. Patel in Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey. Prior to joining Rutgers, he finished his M.E. and B.E. at Stevens Institute of Technology (2015) and University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (2013).
  • New! [June, 2018] Our work "Polarimetric Thermal to Visible Face Verification via Attribute Preserved Synthesis" was accepted by BTAS 2018.
  • New! [May, 2018] Our work "GP-GAN: Gender Preserving GAN for Synthesizing Faces from Landmarks" was accepted by ICPR 2018.
